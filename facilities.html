Our Facilities

At Care Hospital, we offer a range of state-of-the-art facilities to ensure that our patients receive the best possible care. Below is an overview of our primary facilities:

Pathology Department

Our Pathology Department is equipped with the latest diagnostic tools to provide accurate and timely results. We offer a wide range of tests including blood tests, urine tests, and histopathology. Our skilled technicians and pathologists ensure high-quality testing and results.

X-Ray Department

The X-Ray Department features advanced imaging technology for detailed and precise diagnostic imaging. We provide a full range of X-ray services including chest X-rays, bone X-rays, and specialized imaging procedures. Our radiologists are trained to interpret images accurately and promptly.

CT-Scan Department

Our CT-Scan Department uses cutting-edge computed tomography technology to deliver high-resolution images for accurate diagnosis. We perform various CT scans including head, abdomen, and chest scans. Our team ensures patient comfort and safety during the procedure.
